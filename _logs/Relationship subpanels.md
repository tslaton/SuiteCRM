  Task 13 is now complete! Here's where to find all the subpanels:

  Properties Detail View:

  - Related Contacts subpanel - Shows all contacts associated with the property
  - Related Transactions subpanel - Shows all transactions for this property
  - Activities and History subpanels (standard SuiteCRM)

  Contacts Detail View:

  - Related Properties subpanel - Shows all properties this contact is associated with
  - Related Transactions subpanel - Shows all transactions this contact is involved in

  Opportunities/Transactions Detail View:

  - Related Property subpanel - Shows the single property linked to this transaction
  - Related Contacts subpanel - Shows all contacts involved in this transaction

  Additional Features:

  - Create Transaction button in Properties detail view (under Actions)
  - Relationship tables store role and commission data for future enhancement
