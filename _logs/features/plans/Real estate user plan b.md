What are the top 10 value-add features you would imagine for this use case? If we need MLS data, assume we will seed the database with mocked data for the 1-week prototype.

Looking at modernizing SuiteCRM for real estate agents, here are the top 10 value-add features that would transform it into a powerful real estate CRM:

## 1. **Property Management Module**
Transform the standard "Products" module into a comprehensive property tracking system with fields for square footage, bedrooms/bathrooms, listing price, property type, MLS ID, and virtual tour links. Include automated status tracking (active, pending, sold) with historical price changes.

## 2. **Mobile-First Property Showing Scheduler**
Real-time calendar integration that allows agents to schedule showings on-the-go, send automated confirmations to clients, and track showing feedback. Include GPS navigation to properties and automated follow-up reminders.

## 3. **Automated Lead Scoring & Distribution**
AI-powered lead scoring based on property preferences, budget range, timeline, and engagement history. Automatically route hot leads to available agents based on expertise (luxury homes, first-time buyers, commercial properties).

## 4. **Transaction Pipeline Visualization**
Interactive Kanban-style board showing deals from initial contact through closing, with automated task creation for each stage (inspection, appraisal, financing approval, closing documents). Include deadline alerts and document checklists.

## 5. **Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) Generator**
One-click CMA reports pulling from your mocked MLS data, showing comparable properties, price trends, and market insights. Export as branded PDFs for client presentations with your brokerage logo and contact info.

## 6. **Client Portal with Property Matching**
Secure client-facing portal where buyers can view their favorited properties, schedule showings, upload documents, and receive automated alerts when new properties matching their criteria hit the market.

## 7. **Commission Calculator & Split Tracker**
Automated commission calculations based on sale price, brokerage splits, and referral fees. Track pending and received commissions with integration to accounting systems. Include year-to-date earnings dashboards.

## 8. **Open House Management System**
Digital sign-in system for open houses (QR code/tablet), automated follow-up campaigns for attendees, and visitor analytics. Include safety features like visitor photo capture and emergency contact alerts.

## 9. **Neighborhood Insights Dashboard**
Interactive maps showing school ratings, crime statistics, walkability scores, and local amenities. Include demographic data and market trends by neighborhood to help agents become area experts.

## 10. **Automated Marketing Campaign Builder**
Pre-built templates for listing announcements, price reductions, and just-sold postcards. Include social media scheduling, email drip campaigns for different buyer personas, and ROI tracking on marketing spend.

Each feature would integrate seamlessly with SuiteCRM's existing contact management, preserving the robust CRM foundation while adding real estate-specific functionality that agents actually need in their daily workflow.
